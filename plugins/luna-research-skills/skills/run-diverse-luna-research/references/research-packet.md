# Research packet contract

Return one compact packet for the assigned coverage cell. Limit the packet to 12 evidence items and about 800 words.

## Assignment

- Coverage cell
- Question or claim tested
- Scope and exclusions
- Freshness cutoff
- Search strategy and source universe

## Evidence ledger

For each item, provide:

1. A stable evidence ID.
2. The exact claim supported, challenged, or left unresolved.
3. A direct canonical URL plus a section, page, table, or paragraph locator when available.
4. Source title, publisher or author, publication or update date, and access date when freshness matters.
5. What the source directly establishes without inference.
6. Source class: primary, official, peer-reviewed, original data, industry, journalism, expert commentary, or other.
7. Independence: original source, independent corroboration, or dependent repetition; identify the likely upstream source when dependent.
8. Confidence: high, medium, or low, with one sentence of rationale.
9. A limitation, contradiction, freshness concern, or missing context.

## Cell synthesis

- Give three to seven findings specific to the assigned cell.
- Label sourced fact, source interpretation, and inference separately.
- Identify likely conflicts with other cells.
- State important missing evidence.
- Recommend one follow-up query only when the gap is material.

## Self-check

- Every material claim maps to a directly supporting evidence ID.
- URLs are canonical and dependent repetitions are marked.
- Dates are present where freshness matters.
- The packet contains no more than 12 evidence items.
- The assignment performed research reads only and changed no files or external state.
